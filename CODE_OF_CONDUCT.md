# Code of conduct

We want Creature to remain a thoughtful, curious, and welcoming place for mathematical, artistic, and technical collaboration.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful of different disciplines, backgrounds, and levels of experience;
- giving specific, constructive feedback;
- crediting sources and collaborators accurately;
- focusing disagreement on ideas and evidence;
- accepting responsibility and repairing harm when mistakes happen.

Harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, deliberate intimidation, and the uncredited appropriation of others' work are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and interactions that violate these standards. Serious or repeated violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

For conduct concerns, use GitHub's private security advisory or contact the maintainer through the public profile without posting sensitive details in an issue.
